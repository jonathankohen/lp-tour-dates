"The Opera Cowgirls blend powerhouse opera vocals with rollicking country charm, delivering a witty, genre-bending show where Puccini and Patsy Cline, Pavarotti and Parton happily share the same stage."

From Puccini to Parton, Three Tenors to The Chicks, Bolognese to Barbeque... **The Opera Cowgirls** deliver a high-spirited, genre-twisting show where Puccini meets Patsy Cline and everyone gets along surprisingly well. This all-female ensemble blends soaring operatic vocals with the twang and toe-tapping joy of country and Americana, reimagining beloved arias and classics with clever, cowgirl-approved arrangements. Armed with guitars, fiddle, accordion, banjo, and harmonies that can knock the dust off any saloon, they serve up music that’s both impressive and delightfully unpretentious.  With quick wit, irresistible charm, and plenty of musical mischief, The Opera Cowgirls create a lively, unexpected experience that leaves audiences grinning from ear to ear.
