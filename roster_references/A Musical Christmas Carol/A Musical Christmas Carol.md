Adapted from the classic Dickens’ tale, A MUSICAL CHRISTMAS CAROL not only comes with all the traditional plot moments but with a new perspective: an all new musical comedy that is truly funny and in the style of a Broadway show. See *Scrooge, The Ghosts, Tiny Tim, Fezziwig* and all the other beloved characters singing, dancing to all new songs written by Dan Kamin & Randal K West and composed by Justin Hill.

With its music fully integrated into the fabric of the show, A MUSICAL CHRISTMAS CAROL guarantees a joyful, crowd-pleasing holiday celebration for all ages.
