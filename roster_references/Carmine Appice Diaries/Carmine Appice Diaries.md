As the drummer for Vanilla Fudge, Carmine Appice began a legendary career that blazed a path for the most notable rock and roll performers in rock history. After 60 years in the music industry, Carmine is bringing the behind-the-scenes story from his life to your stage. This interactive show will have your audience transfixed by the first-hand tales from Carmine’s life that provide a window into some of the most private corners of rock history.
