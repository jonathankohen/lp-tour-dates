Called the *Creole Queen* by the *British Press*, MORGANE JI is a recording artist who brings to the stage her own rock compositions in explosive performances, blending *Electronic World Music* with deeply rooted African, French, Indian and Asian music.

Her immediately recognizable voice ranges from soft to raspy to animal-like. Melodic lines and shamanic cries intertwine, connecting ancient tribal mysticism with today's stories.

The four-piece band has performed around the world, from Europe to Australia.
