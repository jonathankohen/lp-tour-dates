After his unforgettable performance on Broadway praised by the critics and adored by the public, Academy Award-nominated actor Chazz Palminteri continues to tour this one-man play A BRONX TALE. The story is set in the Bronx, in the mid-60s. It depicts a young boy’s rough childhood and the unforgettable mobsters he encountered as he witnesses a murder. A classic coming-of-age story about reaching one’s true potential and trusting one’s heart, A BRONX TALE was written by Palminteri and directed by four-time Tony Award-winner Jerry Zaks
