LEGENDS OF POP IN CONCERT pays tribute to 3 of the greatest icons of Pop: Madonna, Michael Jackson, and Prince. Headlining are tribute artists Coty Alexander, Brandon Michael Jones, and Bobby Miller, each #1 in their respective impersonation.

The concert offers great flexibility in size and length, ranging from 3 full consecutive 90-minute concerts ideal for festivals to 45-minute performances for private events. Along with the 3 major tribute artists, performances include a band and dancers.

**COTY ALEXANDER IS MADONNA**

The show features different eras and iconic performances from Madonna's 40-year career, including costumes as seen in her Music videos and Tours.   Named #1 Madonna tribute artist of Las Vegas and known for her authentic look, dance abilities, and vocal quality, COTY ALEXANDER has appeared on TV shows including *Good Morning America*, *The Ellen DeGeneres Show,*and*E!* and mentioned in *Rolling Stone, The LA Times, The New York Times,* and *The San Francisco Chronicle*.

The show has performed at major corporate events including *Pepsi, Blue Cross, Travelocity, Abbvie, Bank of America, Sam's Club, Hewlett Packard, IBM, Kimberly Clark*, and more. 

The show features up to 6 dancers with a band and can run from 15 to 90 minutes. The show may be scaled down to a solo act with tracks.

**BRANDON JONES IS MICHAEL JACKSON**

Hailing from the vibrant city of Chicago, BRANDON JONES is electrifying stages around the world with his powerhouse tribute to the one and only Michael Jackson. A born performer, Brandon began captivating audiences at just six years old—his rare talent and undeniable stage presence setting the foundation for what would become an extraordinary career.

Today, he stands as one of the most dynamic and authentic Michael Jackson tribute artists in the industry. Brandon doesn’t just perform the hits—he recreates the magic. Every spin, every note, every iconic move is delivered with precision, passion, and heart, immersing audiences in a thrilling celebration of the King of Pop’s legendary legacy.

Brandon has earned rave reviews for his explosive energy and striking accuracy. Since 2014, he has wowed audiences nationwide as a featured headliner with *Legends in Concert*—the world-renowned live tribute show—bringing the music, moves, and unmistakable magic of Michael Jackson to premier stages across the United States.

But Brandon Jones is more than a tribute artist—he is a complete entertainer. A gifted singer, songwriter, and choreographer, he infuses each performance with his own creative brilliance while remaining deeply respectful to the icon he honors. The result? A show-stopping, high-energy spectacle that leaves audiences on their feet and wanting more.

**BOBBY MILLER IS PRINCE**

This high-energy show captures the spirit and style of the legendary musician, featuring dazzling costumes, spot-on music renditions of Prince's hits, and sizzling choreography.

BOBBY MILLER, one of America's top tribute artist, emulates Prince's persona, voice, and dance moves, recreating the artist's concert experience with talented musicians and dancers.
