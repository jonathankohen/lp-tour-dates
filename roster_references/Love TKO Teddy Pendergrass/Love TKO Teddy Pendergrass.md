Teddy Pendergrass was more than just a singer—he was a legend. With his unmistakable voice, electrifying stage presence, and timeless hits, he defined an era of R&B and soul music. Now, his legacy lives on through LOVE TKO: The Official Teddy Pendergrass Tribute, personally endorsed by the Pendergrass estate.

This extraordinary band features four of Teddy’s original musicians—Robert “WAWA” Legrand, Bill Jolly, Brett Jolly, and Johnny Croom—joined by the powerhouse backing vocals of Toni Trower. At the forefront is dynamic vocalist Damon Keith Williams, who channels the passion, soul, and energy that made Teddy’s music unforgettable.
Audiences will be transported by soul-stirring renditions of “Turn Off the Lights,” “Love T.K.O.,” “Close the Door,” and many more. With the authentic sound and spirit of Teddy Pendergrass, this tribute delivers an experience that transcends nostalgia—it’s a celebration of a true musical icon.

Whether you’re a lifelong fan or discovering Teddy’s music for the first time, LOVE TKO: The Official Teddy Pendergrass Tribute is a must-see experience that honors the man, the music, and the magic.
