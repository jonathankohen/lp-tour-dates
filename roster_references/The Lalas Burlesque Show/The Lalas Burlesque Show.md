Classy, humoristic, and sexy, THE LALAS is the best Burlesque show in the business. Smart and creative, the women of THE LALAS rock the stage and are nothing short of captivating.

Led by celebrity choreographer Erin Lamont (she's worked with Lady Gaga, among many others), THE LALAS comes directly from Los Angeles. Ideal for casinos and cabaret audiences, the show is 75 minutes of perfectly choreographed dance moves set to classic rock & blues music.

With jaw dropping routines and beautiful contemporary dancing elements, THE LALAS' tassel-twirling beauties take burlesque to a whole new level. Each one is an accomplished professional dancer with a career in films, television, commercials, and stage. Their routines are equal parts sultry striptease and athletic showmanship with so much fringe, you’ll wonder how they got it all on the plane.
