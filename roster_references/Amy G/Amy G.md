Olivier-Award winning singer and comedian, Amy G takes on the James Bond songbook, and the icon of the double-oh-douche himself.  This sumptuous and silly, spy comedy concert is a feminist thrill ride, a fiercely smart and funny show, that glorifies the mistreated Bond Girl with epic video art, sharp stand-up and personal stories, hilarious crowd work, and the best music in cinema history.

Can be performed solo, with a live band, or orchestra!
