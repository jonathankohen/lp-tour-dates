**IKONS OF ROCK** brings together a group of seasoned professionals with a shared passion for classic rock. Originally formed as a creative outlet during breaks from touring, the band has grown into an international touring production that faithfully recreates the energy, sound, and look of some of the greatest rock icons in history.

This is not simply a tribute band. **IKONS OF ROCK** is a full-scale classic rock experience. Each performance is carefully crafted — the music is perfected, the stage presence is authentic, and the attention to detail allows audiences to feel as though they are watching multiple legendary artists in their prime. The group’s ability to capture not only the music but also the mannerisms, voices, and visual style of rock’s most celebrated figures has earned them a strong reputation and loyal following.

**IKONS OF ROCK** has shared the stage with artists such as **The Beach Boys, Heart, Cheap Trick, Rick Springfield, Starship, Whitesnake, Bret Michaels, Sebastian Bach, and The Guess Who**, among others. Their international tours consistently draw rave reviews from audiences and presenters alike.

In addition to their tribute performances, the band is also supporting their 2023 original release, *Snake Oil*, which was launched on a European record label.

For presenters and venues, **IKONS OF ROCK** offers a reliable, engaging production that connects with audiences across generations. It is a show that combines familiarity with spectacle — a performance that feels both nostalgic and fresh, and one that delivers strong results at the box office.
