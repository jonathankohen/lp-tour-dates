Tormented by the remnants of many failed relationships, Lolo is tempted by the invitation from a British guy who could be her ticket to a happier life. He’s different. He takes showers. He hasn’t asked her for money. He didn’t bring a stripper on any of their dates. But his attempts to woo her end up triggering a traumatic trip through "boyfriend's past..."

*Lolo’s Boyfriend Show* is a quirky young woman’s crusade to find "true love," which unwittingly leads her to find her "true self." Irreverent, soul-baring and hilarious, the show captivates audiences**with a carousel of characters (all performed by Lauren O'Brien), numerous costume changes on stage and an iconic set with projections.

*Lolo’s Boyfriend Show* won the Edinburgh Fringe IC Audience Favorite Award and was shortlisted for Best Production. Lolo also won the Audience Choice Award at the 2024 NYC Fringe Festival and rocked Off-Broadway at Playhouse 46.
