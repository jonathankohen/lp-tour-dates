Step back into the swinging ’60s with THE MONKEE MEN, a high-energy tribute celebrating the legendary band *The Monkees*. In their peak year of 1967, The Monkees outsold The Beatles and The Rolling Stones combined. They earned two Emmy Awards for their groundbreaking TV show, released five multi-platinum albums, and scored six Billboard Top 10 singles—including three #1 hits.

The Monkee Men band is made up of multi-talented musicians, actors, and singers—hand-picked from the best performers across the country. They perform all the classic Monkees songs live on stage, including *Last Train to Clarksville*, *Pleasant Valley Sunday*, *Daydream Believer*, *I’m a Believer*, and many more.

But this show is more than a concert—it’s an experience... It's a party! Every performance features a crowd-pleasing dance-off, a ’60s-themed costume contest, and a one-of-a-kind moment where an audience member is invited onstage to be serenaded by “Davy.” Fun, interactive, and packed with nostalgia, THE MONKEE MEN deliver a feel-good show that’s perfect for festivals, theaters, casinos, corporate events, and private parties.

THE MONKEE MEN was created by writer/musician/vocalist Ron McNeil, founder of the Emmy Award-winning *Beatles* tribute band, *The Fab Four*.
