**ABOUT THE ARTIST**

Meet Brandon Ethridge, the pianist, conductor, composer, and Broadway musician behind the magic of Musical Memes! With a globe-trotting music career that spans various styles and roles, he has worked alongside Brian May and Roger Taylor of Queen, developing the musical arrangements for the official tribute band of Queen, and with Andrew Lloyd Webber, developing the Broadway musical, School of Rock. In addition to working on Broadway, he has served as music director on dozens of musical theater productions across Germany, Switzerland, and Austria. Currently, he is the music director and pianist for One Vision Of Queen, bringing the iconic music of Queen to life in over 40 countries.
