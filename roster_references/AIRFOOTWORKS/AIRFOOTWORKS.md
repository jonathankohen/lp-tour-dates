AIR FOOTWORKS was created by choreographer and performer, Takashi Jonishi. The show takes the audience on a journey where dance meets zero gravity meets art with amazing precision and synchronization.

The original and unique style of gymnastic-like pole dance combined with Poppin', Robot, and breakdance creates unforgettable performances where dancers appear to be walking on air.

AIR FOOTWORKS provides solo to group performance depending on budget, stage condition and availability.****
