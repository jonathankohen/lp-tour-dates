Alexander Markov’s thrilling new concert tour, BAROQUE TO ROCK, bridges centuries of music with a high-voltage fusion of baroque, classical, and romantic masterpieces supercharged by the explosive energy of rock, including a few Markov original compositions. It’s a genre-defying showcase of the virtuosity and creativity that have made Alexander Markov one of the world’s most captivating violinists.

BAROQUE TO ROCK recently packed the house at Carnegie Hall, which led to an extensive series of international engagements across Europe, Mexico, South America, Asia, and the Middle East.

With Alexander Markov at its helm, BAROQUE TO ROCK captivates classical purists, rock enthusiasts, and new audiences alike, proving that great music—no matter its origin—can thrill, inspire, and unite.

The concert can be adapted to suit any venue, ranging from full symphony orchestras with local choirs to intimate piano duets.
