**“Legends Of Classic Rock — Platinum-selling Rockers delivering #1 hits and arena anthems to audiences worldwide.”**

**Legends Of Classic Rock** is a powerhouse supergroup featuring former members of **Great White, Quiet Riot, White Lion, Alice Cooper, David Lee Roth, and Winger**. Together, they bring their own iconic hits to life along with classics from the legendary artists they’ve worked alongside.

Collectively, the members have sold **over 30 million albums**, earned a **#1 Billboard album**, scored **12 Billboard Hot 100 hits**, and received a **Grammy nomination**. They’ve had **#1 videos on MTV**, appeared on countless magazine covers, and performed to audiences of **30,000–200,000+ fans worldwide**.

In 2025, the band was featured in a **national Starbucks campaign** for the launch of “Iced Energy,” followed by an appearance on **Late Night with Seth Meyers**. The campaign went viral, with memes and clips racking up millions of views across social media. Members have also been spotlighted in a **critically acclaimed Showtime documentary praised by Rolling Stone and ran for two years**, have been portrayed as characters on **The Simpsons**, and even featured on the Top 30 Nielsen-rated show **Full House**.

Since 2022, **Legends of Classic Rock** has performed **300+ shows worldwide**, delivering what they proudly call: *“Real Rock Songs by Real Rock Stars.”*
