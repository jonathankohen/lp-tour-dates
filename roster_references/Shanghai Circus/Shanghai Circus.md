From breathtaking acrobatics to colorful dance, comedy, mystifying magic, and impressive aerial feats of strength, the SHANGHAI CIRCUS offers a truly unforgettable experience. With the incorporation of stage LED technology, audiences can expect an even higher level of entertainment. The show celebrates *Shanghai* culture and entertainment.

For more than 25 years, SHANGHAI CIRCUS has been a resident show at the*Mickey Gilley* Grand Shanghai Theatre, in Branson, MO, and has been touring the US, performing at prestigious venues like the *Hollywood Bowl* and on *Broadway*.

SHANGHAI CIRCUS has also been organizing and participating in many events and activities, such as *Theme Parks‘* summer shows and *Chinese New Year’s Gala* shows to name a few.

The format of the show is flexible and can be customized to any venue.
