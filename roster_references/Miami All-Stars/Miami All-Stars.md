The Miami All Stars is a world-renowned ballroom Dance Company under the direction of choreographers Manny and Lory Castro. They exploded on the scene in 2003 and are passionately dedicated to bringing the “golden age of dance” to the masses. Seen by millions, The Miami All Stars have appeared on Jennifer Lopez’ World of Dance, NBC’s America’s Got Talent, Telemundo’s Gran Opportunidad, and Paula Abdul’s Live to Dance to name a few! The group's multicultural background inspires their tremendous versatility and variety of performance styles. From Cha Cha to Tango and Swing to Salsa, this wild, vivacious group electrifies the stage!
