Endorsed by *Dolly Parton*, ALWAYS CELINE is a powerful, theatrical concert experience celebrating the unforgettable music of Celine Dion. More than a tribute, it’s a moving live performance that captures the heart, passion, and soaring emotion that have defined Dion’s music for generations.

At the center of the production is acclaimed vocalist LAKIETA BAGWELL-GRAVES, whose commanding voice and emotional depth bring these iconic songs to life. Rather than impersonating, LAKIETA delivers each performance with her own artistry—honoring the originals while creating a fresh, authentic concert experience.

Backed by a dynamic live band and enhanced by professional theatrical staging, ALWAYS CELINE takes audiences on a journey through breathtaking ballads and uplifting anthems. Every moment is designed to resonate—from the quiet intimacy of heartfelt classics to the electrifying energy of fan favorites.

Created specifically for performing arts centers and theaters, the production blends live music with polished lighting, rich sound, and integrated visual elements to deliver a fully immersive show. The result is a high-quality concert experience that feels both grand and deeply personal.
